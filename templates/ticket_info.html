Information Regarding Tickets

Where Can I Pay For My Ticket?

To pay for a ticket you can visit the nearest court house as long as your citation does not involve DWI (Driving While Influenced), BAC (Blood Alcohol Content), or a car accident. You also have the option to pay online here.

I Have Lost My Ticket and Need To Find My Court Date. Where Do I Go?

To look up a ticket you can visit our main page and search for by license number. There you will be able to find your court date and any other information you might need.

What Rights Am I Entitled To?

As a citizen you are entitled to a variety of rights to protect your individual liberties. A list of those rights can be found in the PDF found here.

How Can I Contact Someone With Other Questions?

A page provides those who wish to contact someone with questions here via phone number or email during the week depending on court house and hours. To find a court house near you go here.
