Warrants

What To Do If I Have A Warrant?

If there is a warrant out for your arrest you should contact an attorney, arrange to be processed, and be brought in with the attorney. If you do not know if there is a warrant out for your arrest, you can check here.

Is There Any Way To Pay Off A Warrant/Receive Probation?

Any arrangements must be made by a visit to the nearest courthouse. Find a court house here.

Can I Be Arrested If I Come To See A Judge?

Defendants with outstanding warrants are subject to arrest at any time. Individuals are encouraged to appear in court to take care of their tickets and generally are not arrested.

How Can I Contact Someone With Other Questions?

A page provides those who wish to contact someone with questions here via phone number or email durring the week. It will change from court house to court house which can be located here here.
